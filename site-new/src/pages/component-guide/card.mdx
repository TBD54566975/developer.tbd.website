## Card Component

<div className="component-guide text-white">
<p>Generic Card component</p>

## Usage

```jsx
<Card
  title="Sample Title"
  image="https://example.com/image.png"
  text="This is the body text for the card."
  buttonText="Click Me"
  url="/sample-page"
  orientation="horizontal"
  size="medium"
/>
```
