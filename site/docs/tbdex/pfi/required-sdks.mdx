# Required SDKs

To implement and operate as a PFI, you’ll need to use the following SDKs:

- **@web5/dids** - To work with [Decentralized Identifiers (DID)](/docs/web5/decentralized-identifiers/what-are-dids), including creating an identity, and performing any other required cryptographic functions, you’ll need this package. Check out [Key Management Service](/docs/web5/decentralized-identifiers/key-management) for DIDs in production environments.

- **@web5/credentials** - Enable verification of your customer information, including information such as customer’s identity or whether a customer is on a sanctions list. [Verifiable Credentials (VC)](/docs/web5/verifiable-credentials/what-are-vcs) provide a decentralized and trustworthy way of verifying a customer.

- **@tbdex/http-server** - Enable convenience methods for setting up routing and networking to facilitate your PFI.
