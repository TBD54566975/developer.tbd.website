# Welcome to Our Contributor Community!
We're thrilled that you're interested in becoming a part of our thriving community. We believe in fostering an environment where everyone feels welcome and is able to contribute. To ensure the well-being of our community, we adhere to a [Code of Conduct](https://github.com/TBD54566975/developer.tbd.website/blob/main/CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md). Please take a moment to read it thoroughly before you make any contributions.

Before you begin working on an issue, please familiarize yourself with the project's contribution guidelines. This will provide you with an understanding of the process they'd like you to follow and where to ask questions.

## How Can You Contribute?
There are numerous ways to contribute to TBD. Here are some suggestions to get you started. If you have any questions or need help, feel free to reach out to us on our [Discord](https://discord.gg/tbd).

* **Stars on GitHub:** If you resonate with our mission and believe in a decentralized web, consider starring our projects on GitHub! ðŸŒŸ
* **Ask Questions:** Your questions not only help us improve but also benefit the community. If you have a question, don't hesitate to ask it in our [Discord server](https://discord.gg/tbd).
* **Participate in Community Events:** We host a variety of community events on our Discord stage every month, ranging from Show & Tells to brainstorming sessions. Got an event idea or wish to host one? Post it in our [#community-events forum](https://discord.com/channels/937858703112155166/1101192919706255430). We also host DWN office hours every Tuesday, checkout our events calendar in Discord.
* **Propose an Incubation Project:** We're always looking for new incubation projects. Discuss your idea with the community in our Discord or visit our [Technical Proposal repo](https://github.com/TBD54566975/technical-proposals). When you're ready, fork our [project template](https://github.com/TBD54566975/tbd-project-template) and get started. 
* **Improve Documentation:** Good documentation is key to the success of any project. You can help improve the quality of our existing docs or add new ones.
* **Showcase Your Work:** Working on a project or written a blog recently? Share it with the community in our [#share-what-you-do channel](https://discord.com/channels/937858703112155166/1098207585661878402).
* **Give Feedback:** We're always looking for ways to improve. If you have feedback on the website or the docs section, feel free to use our [feedback template](https://github.com/TBD54566975/developer.tbd.website/issues/new/choose).
* **Give Shoutouts:** Is there a project you love or a community/TBD member who's been especially helpful? Feel free to give them a shoutout.
* **Spread the Word:** Help us reach more people by sharing [TBD's repository](https://github.com/TBD54566975), [website](https://developer.tbd.website/docs/), [Twitter](https://twitter.com/TBDDev), and projects.
* **Contribute to the Codebase:** We welcome any and all contributors to help us push the web forward by building a decentralized ecosystem.
